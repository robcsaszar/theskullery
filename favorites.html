If you want to export your favorites to a different browser, or simply want to include your liked recipes from other browsers, please do so below.
Exporting will copy your favorites directly to your clipboard, while pasting the string in the input area and submitting will add them to your local storage and you'll have access to your favorites on other browsers, as well.
No data is ever deleted, so any favorites you already have will stay there.
If you want to purge all favorites from this browser, please scroll to the bottom of the page.
