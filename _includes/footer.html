About

theSkullery.net is all about simplicity, you do not have to log in to see the recipes, your browser is keeping tabs on your favorites, you can dynamically scale your recipes up or down, you can tick off where you are with your instructions, and you can copy all unchecked ingredients to your clipboard for when you go shopping.
I hope you find your inspiration here and cook something as amazing as you.
